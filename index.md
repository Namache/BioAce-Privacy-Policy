# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 30, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Ace Education** ("we", "us", or "our") accesses, collects, stores,
uses, and shares your personal information when you use **BioAce**, our mobile application that
teaches biology through a game-style roadmap, practice activities, and achievements (the "Services").

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Services. If you have questions, contact
us at **ace.education.technologies@gmail.com**.

---

## Summary

- **What we collect:** your name/username and email (via Google Sign-In), the avatar you pick, your
  learning progress and gamification data (streaks, XP, achievements), friend connections and the
  in-app pings you send, any feedback you submit, and basic device/diagnostic data.
- **Sensitive data:** we do **not** collect sensitive personal information (e.g. race, religion,
  sexual orientation, health, precise location).
- **Ads:** the free tier shows occasional **rewarded ads via Google AdMob**, which uses an
  advertising identifier. The paid plan removes ads.
- **Analytics & stability:** we use **Google Analytics for Firebase** and **Firebase Crashlytics**
  to understand usage and fix crashes.
- **Children:** BioAce is intended for users **aged 13 and older**. We do not knowingly collect data
  from children under 13.
- **Your control:** you can edit your profile, and you can **permanently delete your account and all
  associated data from within the app** (Settings → Privacy → Delete account).

---

## 1. Information we collect

### Information you provide
- **Account information** — when you sign in with Google, we receive your **email address** and
  **name** from Google. You also choose a **username** and **avatar** in the app.
- **Feedback & support** — if you use the in-app feedback form, we collect the **name, device
  description, email, and message** you submit.

### Information created as you use the app
- **Learning & gamification data** — lessons read, quiz/practice results, streaks, XP, ranks,
  achievements, weekly quests, treasure-chest claims, and unlocked items.
- **Social data** — friend requests/connections and the lightweight pings (claps, pokes, preset
  messages) you exchange with friends.

### Information collected automatically
- **Device & diagnostic data** — device model, manufacturer, operating-system version, app version,
  general configuration, and crash/diagnostic logs.
- **Advertising identifier** — for users who see ads, Google AdMob may access your device's
  advertising ID.
- **Usage analytics** — aggregated, event-level usage of app features (e.g. lessons completed,
  screens viewed) via Google Analytics for Firebase.

We do **not** collect information about you from third parties, and we do **not** collect sensitive
personal information.

## 2. How we use your information

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Services (your account, progress, and social features).
- Personalize your experience and save your progress across devices.
- Show ads on the free tier and, where required, request your consent for them.
- Measure and improve the app (analytics) and detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and bugs.
- Respond to your feedback and support requests.
- Maintain security and prevent fraud or abuse.
- Comply with legal obligations.

We process your information only when we have a valid legal reason to do so.

## 3. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we rely on: **performance of a contract** (to run your account and
the Services), **consent** (for advertising and analytics where required, which you can withdraw),
**legitimate interests** (to keep the app secure and improve it), and **legal obligations**.

## 4. Third-party services we share data with

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited data with the service providers below,
who process it on our behalf:

| Provider | Purpose | What it may process |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Sign-In** | Authentication | Email, name, Google account ID |
| **Supabase** | Backend, database & hosting of your account/progress | Account, profile, progress, social, and feedback data |
| **Google AdMob** | Rewarded ads (free tier) | Advertising ID, device & ad-interaction data |
| **Google Analytics for Firebase** | Usage analytics | App events, device & app identifiers |
| **Firebase Crashlytics** | Crash & stability reporting | Crash logs, device & diagnostic data |

Each provider handles data under its own privacy terms. For Google services, see the
[Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy) page. We may also disclose information
if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

## 5. Advertising and your choices

The free tier shows occasional **rewarded ads** (you choose to watch them in exchange for in-app
rewards) served by **Google AdMob**. Ads may use your device's advertising identifier.

- **Remove ads:** subscribe to the paid plan, which disables ads.
- **Limit ad personalization:** use your device settings (Android → Settings → Google → Ads) to
  reset or delete your advertising ID and opt out of ad personalization.
- **EEA/UK:** where required, we ask for your consent before showing personalized ads and respect
  your choice.

## 6. Analytics and crash reporting

We use **Google Analytics for Firebase** and **Firebase Crashlytics**. You can opt out of Google
Analytics across the web via the [Google Analytics opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
Crash reports contain diagnostic data, not the contents of your lessons or messages.

## 7. Children's privacy

BioAce is intended for learners **aged 13 and older**, and the app asks you to confirm you are 13 or
older before use. We do **not** knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you
believe a child under 13 has provided us personal information, contact us at
**ace.education.technologies@gmail.com** and we will delete it.

## 8. How long we keep your information

We keep your personal information only as long as needed to provide the Services and for the purposes
described here, unless a longer period is required by law. When you delete your account, your profile
and associated data are removed (see Section 10).

## 9. How we keep your information safe

We use reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect your information, including
encrypted (HTTPS) connections and access controls on our backend. No method of transmission or
storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

**United States / California:** we do **not sell** your personal information, and we do not share it
for cross-context behavioral advertising beyond the ad services described above. You may exercise the
rights described here regardless of where you live.
